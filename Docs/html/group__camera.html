Creates an orthographic camera.

Parameters
positionThe position of the camera in world units.
targetThe target of the camera in world units.
upThe up vector of the camera. This does not need to be accurate as it gets recalculated internally.
heightThe height of the viewport, in world units. The width is automatically adjusted based on the aspect ration
nearThe near field of the camera. This is not necessary, but is provided as an extra parameter.
farThe far field of the camera. This is not necessary, but is provided as an extra parameter.

Creates a thinlens camera. This is essentially the perspective camera with a predefined lens radius and focal distance.

Parameters
positionThe position of the camera in world units.
targetThe target of the camera in world units.
upThe up vector of the camera. This does not need to be accurate as it gets recalculated internally.
apertureThe vertical FOV of the camera.
farThe far field of the camera. This is not necessary, but is provided as an extra parameter.
lens_radiusThe spherical radius of the thinlens aperture. Setting this to 0 delegates the thinlens camera to the perspective pinhole camera.
focal_distanceThe focal distance of the camera, the plane where the objects are in focus. Setting this to 0 delegates the thinlens camera to the perspective pinhole camera.
